## Exercise 2.26

Suppose we define x and y to be two lists:

```scheme
(define x (list 1 2 3))
(define y (list 4 5 6))
```

What result is printed by the interpreter in response to evaluating each of the following expressions:

```scheme
(append x y)
(cons x y)
(list x y)
```

**Ans**

```scheme
> (define x (list 1 2 3))
> (define y (list 4 5 6))
> (append x y)
'(1 2 3 4 5 6)
> (cons x y)
'((1 2 3) 4 5 6)
> (list x y)
'((1 2 3) (4 5 6))
```
